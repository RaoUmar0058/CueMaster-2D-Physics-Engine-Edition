Privacy Policy — CueMaster 2D

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

CueMaster 2D ("the App") is a casual offline arcade game developed by Codensia. This policy explains what information the App does and does not collect.

Data Collection

CueMaster 2D does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App does not require an internet connection to play, does not use accounts or logins, and does not access contacts, location, camera, microphone, or any other device permissions.

Local Game Data

Score, coins, and progress are stored only on your own device and are never sent to any server. Uninstalling the App removes this data permanently.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs at this time. If this changes in a future update, this policy will be updated accordingly before that update is released.

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

Any future changes to this policy will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
